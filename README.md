    _ RULES OF THE BOSTON DISPENSARY FOR THE MEDICAL RELIEF OF THE POOR.

      * Form of a RECEIPT given to each CONTRIBUTOR by the TREASURER. Boſton Diſpenſary

      * AT a Meeting of the MANAGERS of the BOSTON DISPENSARY, March 10, 1797.

    _ THE following Gentlemen were choſen MANAGERS of the Boſton Diſpenſary, the ſecond Thurſday in January 1797, to continue in office one year.
